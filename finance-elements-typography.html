h1 Heading

This is example of standard h2 heading. The best practice for SEO purposes is to have one h1 heading on your page. For example you can use it on your page title, like we did in this template.

h2 Heading

This is example of standard h2 heading. It is used for all major headings on your page. For example the page name is Services. This will be h1 heading. After that you have specific service called "Consulting". This will be h2 heading.

h3 Heading

This is example of h3 heading. According to our previous heading standard, on services page you have main service called "Consulting". Then of course you have different fields of consulting, like "Tourism Consulting". That will be heading h3.

h4 Heading

This is example of standard h4 heading. It is used for less important headlines. The basic rule is to follow hierarchy. So for example you have service "Tourism Consulting", like we mentioned previously. If you have different services for that main service and you are showing them on that same page, the heading of that services should be h4.

h5 Heading

This is example of standard h5 heading. It is used for some small elements on the page, less important elements. Where you will use it will depend on structure of your page and how many main sections and subsections you have.

h6 Heading

This is example of standard h6 heading. It is used for some small elements on the page, less important elements. Where you will use it will depend on structure of your page and how many main sections and subsections you have.
