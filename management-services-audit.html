Audit and Assurance

In rapid growing market, right audit is very important. ConsultingPress will help you enhance the value of your business by right guidance based on wide industry experience.

ConsultingPress will help you create more value from your business and mitigate risk. ConsultingPress will help you in financial reporting, accounting and revenue recognition.

Activities and results:

  • Financial Reporting

    Financial reporting has specific requirement depending on your business location. ConsultingPress will help you solve and deal with complexity of financial reporting.

  • Accounting advisory

    Weather you are global, international company or a small business owner, ConsultingPress will help you recognize the risks and opportunities specific for your business.

    ConsultingPress will guide you through new revenue recognition standard, that will take effect on all industry sectors in US.
