Position summary

Here at ConsultingPress we are always looking for fresh talents to join our team. We are looking for online marketing specialist in London UK in Vulnerability Management department. Please read carefully what we require and what we offer and use our online form for submission.

  • Location: London, UK

  • Department: Vulnerability Management

  • Compensation: $85 000/year

Responsibilities

  • Content management and development including writing specific content for our website like blog posts, case studies, etc.

  • Managing communication with partner agencies and implementation of IT Security plans

  • Communication with clients

  • Implementation of online marketing campaigns

Requirements

  • MBA in IT Security or related fields

  • Passion for IT Security consulting, management and marketing

  • Strong communication and written skills

  • Strong attention to details and ability to solve complex problems

  • Team player

How to apply

To apply for this job position please use the form below, or if you find it more suitable please send us an email on info@consultingpress.com and include your CV.
