Tourism development

Tourism is one of the largest and fastest growing industries in the world. It plays a vital role in smaller and less developed countries as it creates business opportunities and have positive impact on economy and life of local people.

Here at ConsultingPress we consider sustainable tourism a powerful tool that optimizes natural, economical and social aspect of tourism. That's why we are focused to sustainable tourism development, that creates positive experiences for both local people and travelers.

Why sustainable tourism?

First let's talk about sustainable tourism meaning. Sustainable tourism doesn't mean only eco-friendly or green tourism. It's much more than that. Investing in sustainable tourism you are developing environmental, economic and socio-cultural aspect of your tourism destination. Sustainable tourism helps in preserving cultural heritage, natural resources and helps in economy growth.
